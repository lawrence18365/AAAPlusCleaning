Kellyville: Sydney's Most Sought-After Suburb

Located 36 kilometers northwest of Sydney's CBD in the prestigious Hills District, Kellyville has emerged as Sydney's most popular suburb for house purchases in 2023. This thriving community combines modern development with a rich historical heritage, offering residents an ideal blend of suburban comfort and urban connectivity.

A Legacy of Growth: Historical Perspectives

Named after Hugh Kelly, a former convict who arrived in New South Wales in 1803, Kellyville's transformation began when his land was subdivided into farmlets after his passing in 1884. The suburb's history features notable milestones including the establishment of Kellyville Public School in 1849 and the opening of the Post Office in 1889.

Education and Learning

Kellyville provides excellent educational opportunities through:

  • Kellyville Public School - established 1849
  • Our Lady of the Rosary Primary School
  • Various preschool options
  • Close proximity to university facilities

Housing and Development

As Sydney's most popular suburb for house purchases, Kellyville recorded 677 house sales in one year with a median price of $1.22 million. The area features larger block sizes compared to neighboring suburbs and showcases modern housing estates designed for family living.
